Meet the O'Ferals

A close-knit family of stray cats living in the charming town of Skerries, Ireland. Whiskers O'Feral — fun-loving patriarch and master storyteller — has decided it's time for a big change. He wants to move the family to New York.

His young kitten Gaffle loves their family dearly and feels uncertain about leaving everything he knows behind. To ease his worries, Whiskers paints a bright picture of America — claiming the streets are paved with fish and promising an unforgettable adventure together.

Although hesitant, Gaffle agrees to the move, hoping it will help him find a home. But as he learns his father's hidden motive for the journey, Gaffle begins to question everything — and discovers he's about to become the main character in a wonderful, funny, and inspiring adventure all his own.

About the Author

J.T. Meuse

J.T. Meuse is a debut author residing in Northern California. Her own feral cat, Gaffle, inspired this story — a heartfelt tale that aims to give a voice to millions of beings without a home, both animal and human alike.
